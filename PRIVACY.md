# Privacy

Clip is a local clipboard history app.

## What Clip Stores

Clip stores clipboard history on your own Windows device under:

```text
%LOCALAPPDATA%\Clip
```

That folder can contain copied text, image files, file paths, metadata, app cache data, and logs.

## What Clip Sends

Clip does not intentionally send clipboard history to a server.

## Before Publishing or Sharing Logs

Clipboard history and logs can contain private content. Do not upload your local `%LOCALAPPDATA%\Clip` folder, `history.json`, logs, or screenshots unless you have reviewed them first.
